Part-time Employment 

Summary/Purpose:  Guidelines are given for a person employed on a part-time basis, including 
who approves the hourly rate of pay for non-faculty and who does not qualify for membership in 
the PERS or group health and life insurance program. 

A person employed on a part-time basis (other than members of the faculty who are reported on 
HR Form 1) on an irregular schedule of work shall be paid at an hourly rate which has been 
approved by the appropriate Vice Chancellor and the Department of Human Resources.  

Persons employed less than one-half time and/or less than 4Â½ months are not eligible for 
membership in the Public Employees' Retirement System or group health and life insurance 
program.
