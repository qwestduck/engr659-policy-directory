Hours of Work 
 

Summary/Purpose:  In various departments the schedule and number of work hours may vary.  
All administrative offices are open 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.  Any variation from

In various departments, the schedule and number of work hours vary as required by the nature of 
services performed. If necessary, employees are expected to work overtime when needed.   
 
All administrative offices are open 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.  Any variation from 
this schedule which may seem necessary because of the type of service rendered by a particular 
department must be approved by the Chancellor.
