Adoption of Building Codes, Laws, and Regulations 
 
Summary/Purpose:  The University of Mississippi acknowledges and follows the most recent 
published editions of all applicable construction and life-safety codes. 
 
 
The University of Mississippi shall acknowledge and follow the most recent published editions 
of the International Building Code, International Residential Code, International Plumbing Code, 
International Mechanical Code, International Fuel Gas Code, International Fire Code, National 
Electrical Code, Americans with Disabilities Act Accessibility Guidelines (ADAAG), etc. These 
adopted documents shall be utilized by all relevant design professionals.
