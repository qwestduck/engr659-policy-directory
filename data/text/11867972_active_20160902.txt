Recognition of Constitution Day 
 

Summary/Purpose:  In order to comply with the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2005 (36 

U.S.C.A. ยง 106), the office of the Ole Miss Student Union will organize an annual educational 

event to recognize Constitution Day on September 17.   

 

At minimum, the recognition will include a display area inside the Ole Miss Student Union.  If 

September 17 falls on a weekend or holiday, Constitution Day publicity will occur either the 

preceding or following week as determined by the University.  The Office of the Ole Miss 

Student Union shall maintain a record of the displays or other events commemorating the day.
