Obtaining Ole Miss Express Refunds 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Ole Miss Express Refund request processing information including  
   associated fees, request processing time, and the review process 
 
 
All refund requests must be submitted in writing to the ID Center. Refunds take at least 10-14  
days to process, and are subject to approval. Refunds to students upon graduation will be  
processed without charge. All others require a $20 processing fee which is deducted from the  
member’s account before the refund is applied to the student’s Bursar account.
