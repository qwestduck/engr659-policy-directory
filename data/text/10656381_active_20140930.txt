Design Professional Contracts 
 
Summary/Purpose:  Contracts from all Design Professionals for construction or design projects 
must be signed by the Director of Facilities Planning.  
 
 
Contracts from all Design Professionals for construction or design projects must be signed by the 
Director of Facilities Planning to ensure all applicable codes, the guidelines of the University, 
and the guidelines of the State of Mississippi are followed.
