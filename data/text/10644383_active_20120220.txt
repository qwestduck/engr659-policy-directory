Check Cashing 
 

Summary/Purpose:  The University of Mississippi Bursar’s policy on cashing checks. 

 

 

The Office of the Bursar provides check cashing service to currently enrolled students and 

faculty/staff.  In order to use this service, accounts must be current.  Checks can be cashed up to 

a $125.00 maximum amount with a 50¢ fee per check cashed. 

 

The University of Mississippi reserves the right to withhold check cashing privileges.
