
The University of Mississippi 

Professional Service Fees 
 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Payments for professional services made to individuals or companies 
performing services for the University. 
 
 
Professional fees to University faculty and staff will be paid through the payroll system. See 
Payroll Procedures for detailed instructions. 
 
Professional fee payments are authorized for individuals and companies that have contracted 
with the University to provide professional services.  Only independent contractors, as defined 
by IRS regulations, may be paid under a professional service contract.  See Professional Service 
Fee Procedures for detailed instructions.
