Approval of Lease and Easements 
 
Summary/Purpose:  The Department of Facilities Planning must review all proposed easements 
and leases involving University property before they may be signed. 
 
 
The Facilities Planning Department must be contacted to initiate any lease or easement involving 
University properties in order to avoid conflicts and properly coordinate with existing utilities, 
site boundaries, adjacencies, and the campus master plan, among other considerations.
