Observance of Local, State, and Federal Criminal Laws 

Summary/Purpose: To nurture character development and integrity by informing students of 
their duty to honor established University, local, state, and federal regulations. 

Students at the University of Mississippi have the responsibility to refrain from conduct that 
would violate local, state, or federal criminal laws.  Students violating local, state, and/or federal 
laws may be subject to conduct action in addition to criminal prosecution.
