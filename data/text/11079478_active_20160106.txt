Responsibility for Guests
 
DSA.SC.200.065
 

Summary/Purpose: To inform students of their duty to ensure that their guest honor 
university policy. 

Students at the University of Mississippi have the responsibility to ensure that their guests on 
campus behave in a manner consistent with the community values and policies of the 
University of Mississippi.  Students may be held responsible for the actions of their guests, 
including facing conduct charges, when a guest violates university policy.
