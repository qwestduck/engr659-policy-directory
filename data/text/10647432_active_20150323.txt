Hall Sports 
 

Summary/Purpose: his policy explains that active sports or other similar type activities are not 
permitted to take place inside residence halls.  
 
In order to maintain and environment that is conducive for study, sports are not to be played in 
residence hall areas.  This includes basketball, football, soccer Frisbee, water fights, “tag,” 
“chase,” golfing, wrestling, skateboarding, and skating/rollerblading. Students are encouraged to 
enjoy these activities outdoors, at the Turner Center or through organized intramural activities.
