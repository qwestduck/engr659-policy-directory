Meal Plans Semester Schedule  

Summary/Purpose:  Set up of meal plans per semester  

Unused meals are NOT carried over into the next semester. Meal plan participants are granted two meals per meal period 

and a maximum of six meals per day from their plan. Each meal plan allows ten guest meals, part of the total meals, per 

semester.
