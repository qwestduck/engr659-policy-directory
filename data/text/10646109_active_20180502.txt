Operating Budget 
 

Summary/Purpose:  The University of Mississippi prepares its annual operating budget in the 
spring of each year for the fiscal year that begins on July 1. Specific instructions and deadlines 
for completion of the budget process are provided through the appropriate administrative 
channels. 
 
The fringe benefit rate for budgetary purposes is calculated annually and provided by the Budget 
Office. Fringe benefits are calculated on all non-student salaries and wages.  
  
Each unit is responsible for reviewing all operating category amounts and submitting any 
necessary adjustments between categories during the annual budget process.
