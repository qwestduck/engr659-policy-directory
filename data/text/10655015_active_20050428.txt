
The University of Mississippi 

Gift Solicitation 

Summary/Purpose:  This is a rule that states employees are not to solicit gifts from students and 
students are not to give gifts to faculty and staff.

Based upon the premise that giving should be voluntary, students are not to be solicited for 
contributions or gifts from any employee who has decision-making authority over them, and 
personal gifts from students to faculty and staff are prohibited.
