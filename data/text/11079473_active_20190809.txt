Participating in Student Organizations 
DSA.DS.100.005 

 
Summary/Purpose:  The purpose of this policy is to educate students of their right to participate 
in Registered Student Organizations.  
 
University of Mississippi students have the right to participate in Registered Student 
Organizations (RSOs) and conduct social affairs. The activities of RSOs are governed by policies 
established by the Ole Miss Student Union and the Office of Conflict Resolution and Student 
Conduct. Responsibility for the conduct of activities rests with the sponsoring organization. All 
RSOs are required to adhere to the policies, guidelines, and standards of behavior and conduct 
outlined by the University.
