
The University of Mississippi 

Cable Television 

Summary/Purpose:  Defines the purchases of campus cable television services and use of the 
system.  

Students: 

Basic cable television service provided to residence halls is part of the housing fee. Students can 
subscribe to additional channels individually.  The Bursar will bill students for subscribed services. 
Payment is required within 10 days of receipt of notice from the Bursar. The information provided 
when subscribing for service must be accurate. Service can be cancelled at any time after a 
one-month minimum activation. Refunds, after the one-month minimum, are prorated based on 
days service is used in the month.  

Academic/Administrative Departments: 

Departments can subscribe to basic cable TV services. The subscriber for service must be a 
signatory officer of the cost center billed for services. The information provided when 
subscribing for service must be accurate. Service can be cancelled at any time after a one-month 
minimum activation. Refunds, after the one-month minimum, are prorated based on days service is 
used in the month.   

Notice to All Users: 

Unauthorized reception of cable service is illegal and is covered by the Communications Act 
of 1934 (SEC. 633. (47 U.S.C. 553)) in which it states ¡°(a)(1) No person shall intercept or 
receive or assist in intercepting or receiving any communications service offered over a cable 
system, unless specifically authorized to do so by a cable operator or as may otherwise be 
specifically authorized by law. Any person who willfully violates subsection (a)(1) shall be 
fined not more than $1,000 or imprisoned for not more than 6 months, or both.  

VIOLATIONS OF THIS CODE WILL BE ENFORCED AND HANDLED BY THE 
PROPER UNIVERSITY AUTHORITIES.
