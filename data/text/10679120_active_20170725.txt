Concerts 
 

Summary/Purpose:  This policy is for musical entertainment on campus. 

 

The Ole Miss Student Union reserves the exclusive right to produce and promote concerts on 

campus.  The Ole Miss Student Union understands that other agencies may host musical 

entertainment campus including, but not limited to, Artist Series, Ford Series, L-O-U Summer 

Sunset Series, Ole Miss Athletics, and events at fraternity and sorority houses. No concert of a 

popular music nature, however, shall be produced or promoted on campus without the consent of 

the Ole Miss Student Union.
