I-20 Extension 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Extension of Stay for International Students 
 
International students in F-1 status must apply for an extension of stay prior to the end 
date on the form I-20 if additional time is needed to complete the degree program. 
Extensions must be approved by the International Student Advisor and can only be done 
in specific circumstances as allowed by federal regulations.
