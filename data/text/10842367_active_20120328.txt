Tree Dedications 
 

Summary/Purpose: Defines guidelines for the dedications of trees on campus.  

Trees will be planted in an appropriate place with the names put on a centrally located plaque 
arboretum wall on the west side of Martindale. The cost is $2,000.  All memorial plantings and 
plaque(s) are to be approved by the Facilities Planning Committee.
