Vending Machine Repair 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Contractual Services Management is responsible for reporting any  
   necessary repairs or maintenance to vending machines located on the
   University of  Mississippi campus 
 
 
Contractual Services Management will notify the appropriate vending machine owner of any 
need(s) for maintenance or repair on the University of Mississippi campus.
