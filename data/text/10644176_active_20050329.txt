Eligibility For University of Mississippi ID Card 
 

Summary/Purpose:   Defines those eligible to receive a University of Mississippi  
   official identification card 
 
 
A University of Mississippi ID card is issued only to current students, faculty, and staff. 
All other requests for ID cards must be requested, in writing, to the ID Center and are 
subject to approval.
