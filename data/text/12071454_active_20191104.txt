
The University of Mississippi 
 
 

Hoverboards and Personal Movers 
 
Summary/Purpose:  Establishes guidelines for the possession and operation of hoverboards or 
personal movers on the Oxford Campus of the University of Mississippi. 
 
As a result of safety concerns, the University of Mississippi has prohibited the operation or 
charging of all hoverboards or personal movers in all university buildings or facilities.  In 
addition, possession of hoverboards or personal movers is prohibited in any university residential 
facility.
