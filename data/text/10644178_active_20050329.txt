Identity Verification Requirements 
For University of Mississippi Identification Card 

 
Summary/Purpose:   Insures that correct individual receives official University of 
    Mississippi identification(ID) card 
 
 
Before a University of Mississippi ID card is issued, identification of the cardholder must 
be verified, an ID number assigned, and status at the University of Mississippi 
determined.
