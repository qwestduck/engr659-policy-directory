Summary:  Described a limited list of government functions that may require the

Affected Individuals: Staff at HIPAA covered entities, patients of HIPAA cover entities 

 

1.0 PURPOSE 
 

To  guide  University  of  Mississippi (UM)  employees  in  making disclosures of 
protected health information (PHI) for specialized governmental functions. 

 

 
2.0 SCOPE 

 
The UM Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information for Specialized 
Government Functions Policy applies to any UM employee who releases PHI for 
specialized government functions. 

 
3.1 STANDARDS 

 
UM may disclose PHI for the following purposes without an authorization from the 
individual: 

 

 
  Uni ted State s mi li tar y per son nel : 

UM can use and disclose the PHI of individuals who are Armed Forces 
personnel for activities deemed necessary by appropriate military command 
authorities to assure the proper execution of the military mission, if the 
appropriate military authority has published by notice in the Federal Register the 
following information: 

o Appropriate military command authorities; and 

o The purposes for which the PHI may be used or disclosed. 
 

 

 
  Nati onal Securi ty and In telli gen ce a cti vi ti es:  

UM may disclose PHI to authorized federal officials for the conduct of lawful 
intelligence, counter-intelligence, and other national security activities authorized 
by the National Security Act and implementing authority. 

 
  Pr ote cti ve ser vi ces fo r the Presi dent an d ot her s:  

UM may disclose PHI to authorized federal officials for the provision of protective 
services to the President or other persons authorized by 18 U.S.C. 3056, or the 
foreign heads of state or other person authorized by 22 U.S.C. 2709(a)(3), or for 
the conduct of investigations authorized by 18 U.S.C. 871 and 879.
