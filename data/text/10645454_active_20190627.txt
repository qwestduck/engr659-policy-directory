
The University of Mississippi

Employee Work-Related Injuries

Summary/Purpose: To provide initial and short-term follow-up care for employees who suffer 
from a work-related injury or illness.

The Student Health Service will provide short-term or emergency care to employees who have 
an illness related to work or are injured while on the job.  The Student Health Service will 
evaluate and treat when appropriate.  When necessary, the employees will be referred to the local 
hospital or appropriate specialists.
