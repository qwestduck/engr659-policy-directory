Reactivation of Lost Identification Card 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Prevents possible identity theft using official University of 
   Mississippi identification cards 
 
 
ID’s reported as lost or stolen will not be reactivated over the phone. Only ID’s presented 
in person at the ID Card Center by the cardholder identified on the card will be reinstated 
as an active account.
