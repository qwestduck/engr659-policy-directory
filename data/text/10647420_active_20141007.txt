
The University of Mississippi 

 

COMPUTER USAGE AND WIRELESS NETWORKS 

 

Summary/Purpose: This policy outlines the appropriate use of computers in residence hall 

rooms.  

 

Students wishing to have a computer in their room or apartment are encouraged to provide their own, 

subject to the following policies. The following practices are prohibited:  

  Personal networks (wireless networks, wireless routers, etc.)  

  Viewing Pornography, running a business, etc.  

  Hacking  

  Creating and running servers  

  Using a computer with malicious intent  

  Illegal file sharing
