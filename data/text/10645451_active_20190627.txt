Eligibility for Student Health Service 

Summary/Purpose:  To define Student Health Service care eligibility. 

 
Eligibility for Student Health Service care is available to all currently enrolled students at the 
University of Mississippi, Oxford Campus. Students also are eligible for services between the 
spring and fall Semesters if they completed the previous spring semester and are enrolled for the 
following fall Semester.
