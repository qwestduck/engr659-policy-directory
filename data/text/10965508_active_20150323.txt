Student Owned Furniture 
 

Summary/Purpose:  This policy discusses the guidelines for student owned property and the 
consequences for leaving furniture behind. 
 
Any furniture brought in by the resident must be marked with indelible marker in order to 
identify the owner. At check-out residents are responsible for removing any furniture they may 
have brought in during the year.  Resident will be billed for leaving furniture in their rooms or 
elsewhere in the building.
