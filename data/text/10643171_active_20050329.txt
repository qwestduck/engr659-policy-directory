Full Course of Study for International Students 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Enrollment requirements for international students 
 
In general, international students in F-1 or J-1 status must enroll for a full course of study 
defined as 12 hours for undergraduate students and 9 hours for graduate students during 
the fall and spring semesters. Permission to drop below full time must be obtained from 
the International Student Advisor prior to dropping below full time.
