Permission Required to Use University Trademarks 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Written permission is required to use University of Mississippi  
   trademarks 
 
 
Any individual wishing to utilize any trademark held by the University of Mississippi must 
request permission (in writing) from the Office of Licensing.  If products are created for resale, 
individuals or companies must apply and be granted permission to become a licensee of the 
University of Mississippi.
