
The University of Mississippi 

Nonresident Fee Waiver for Children of Faculty and Staff 
 

Summary/Purpose:  An explanation is given of the qualifications needed to receive a waiver of 
the nonresident fee for children of faculty and staff. 
  
Children of permanent, full-time faculty and staff are eligible for a waiver of the nonresident fee. 
These waivers will be handled through the Office of Financial Aid. The employee must be 
employed as of the first date of the term for which the benefit is provided.
