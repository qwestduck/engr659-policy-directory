Vending Machine Refunds 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Each vendor is responsible for dispensing refunds for products coming  
   from vending machines owned and maintained by them 
 
 
The University of Mississippi is not responsible for dispensing cash refunds to individuals.  Any 
individual requiring a refund should contact the appropriate vendor in order to request a refund.
