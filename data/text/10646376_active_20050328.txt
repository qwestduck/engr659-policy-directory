Bulletin Boards 
 

Summary/Purpose:  This is a statement about the proper use of bulletin boards.   
 
Bulletin Boards controlled by departments and other University offices are intended for non-
commercial use and may be restricted to use for certain purposes--the posting of departmental 
and University announcements, position vacancy announcements, graduate fellowship 
announcements, etc.
