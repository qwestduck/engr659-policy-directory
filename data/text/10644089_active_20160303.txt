Facilities and Administrative Rate on Contracts or Grants 
 

Summary/Purpose:  The University will request and recover full F&A costs for all sponsored 
projects at the applicable federally negotiated indirect cost rate except where limited by the 
respective sponsor.  When situations arise which require a reduced rate or a waiver, the Vice 
Chancellor for Research and Sponsored Programs should be contacted for approval.
