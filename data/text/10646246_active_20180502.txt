Legislative Budget 
 

Summary/Purpose:  The University of Mississippi Legislative Budget Request for each fiscal 
year is prepared in the summer, prior to the start of the legislative session the following January. 
Specific instructions and deadlines for completion of the annual budget request are provided by 
the Budget Office as received from the IHL.  
 
A separate budget request is submitted for On-Campus, Off-Campus, Current Restricted, Current 
Auxiliary Operations, and each separately budgeted unit.
