Charging Ole Miss Express 
To Student Accts Receivable 

 
Summary/Purpose:  Conditions in which Ole Miss Express may be charged to student’s 
   billing 
 
 
Requests to bill Ole Miss Express to a student’s Bursar account are granted only before 
the start of each semester or upon approval through the Office of Financial Aid. Financial 
Aid deferment requests must be submitted through the financial aid website.
