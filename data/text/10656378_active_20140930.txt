Approval of New Projects 
 
Summary/Purpose:  Written approval from the Department of Facilities Planning must be 
obtained for any University construction, renovation, and/or landscaping projects. 
 
 
All new construction, renovation, and landscaping projects at the Oxford campus of the 
University of Mississippi must receive written approval from the Department of Facilities 
Planning before any project related activity may begin. This includes all buildings and grounds at 
the Oxford campus, or those maintained by the University or any of its affiliates including, but 
not limited to, Academic Buildings, Auxiliary Buildings, Athletic Buildings, Fraternity Houses, 
Sorority Houses, Service Buildings, Student Housing Buildings, etc.
