
The University of Mississippi 

Awards 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Awards for scientific, educational, artistic, or literary achievements. 
 
Awards for scientific, educational, artistic, or literary achievements are taxable income to the 
recipient. Awards for faculty and staff must be processed on a Personnel Action form (PA) 
through the university payroll system. This includes outstanding teacher awards and staff 
appreciation awards. 
 
The appropriate general ledger expense code is 55140.
