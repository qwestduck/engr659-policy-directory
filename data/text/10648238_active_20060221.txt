
The University of Mississippi

Address

Summary/Purpose:  The purpose of this policy is to state the approved campus address.

The University administration approved the mandatory use of “Post Office Box 1848" in all
campus addresses.  This expedites the delivery of campus mail and assists Campus Mail Services
in their objective of same day delivery.  The post office box must be included in the address
directly above the city, state and zip code.  The new zip code is “38677-1848".  The
department’s name must also be included in the address.
