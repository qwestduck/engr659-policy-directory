
The University of Mississippi 

Employment-At-Will Statement 
 

Summary/Purpose:  This statement is necessary to ensure that employees realize their 
employment at The University of Mississippi is "at will" and terminable "at will" by the 
University or staff member with or without cause.   
 
Employment at The University of Mississippi is “at will” and terminable “at will” by the 
University or staff member with or without cause.  Any oral and written statements or promises 
to the contrary, other than contracts issued by the Board of Trustees of State Institutions of 
Higher Learning, are not binding upon the University.  Should any such statements be made 
suggesting that employment at this University is other than “at will,” the employee should 
contact the Department of Human Resources.
