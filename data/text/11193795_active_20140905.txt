Violation of Other Applicable University Policies
 
DSA.SC.200.090
 

Summary/Purpose: To provide students notice about their obligation to exercise integrity by 
honoring other stated University of Mississippi Policies. 

Students at The University of Mississippi have the responsibility to comply with all other 
generally applicable University policies including but not limited to Alcohol, Drugs, Tobacco 
Use, and the Information Technology Appropriate Use Policy.
