In accordance with FCC regulations and our cable contract, the splicing or splitting of cable to 

other rooms is not permitted. The University will seek appropriate action and restitution due to 

the splicing of cable by students. FCC regulations dictate that fines of up to $1000 may be 

assessed for each occurrence. Please be aware that radio and TV antennas and satellite dishes 

are not allowed.
