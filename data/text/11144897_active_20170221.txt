IACUC Protocol Animal Adoptions 
 

Summary/Purpose:  Adoption of animals used in IACUC protocols is not permitted. 
 
 
Adoption of animals used in IACUC-approved protocols is not permitted.  
 
Only the IACUC may approve animal adoptions in exceptional cases with sufficient justification.
