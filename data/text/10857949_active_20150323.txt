Apartment Subleasing 
 

Summary/Purpose:  This policy explains the policy against subleasing and gives the 
consequences of policy violation. 
 
 
Subleasing is a violation of a resident’s rental agreement. If a resident is found in violation of 
this policy, the unauthorized occupant(s) will be asked to move out within 24 hours. 
Additionally, the occupant will be fined $50 per day. If the unauthorized person does not vacate 
the apartment within the specified time, the resident’s lease agreement will be terminated.
