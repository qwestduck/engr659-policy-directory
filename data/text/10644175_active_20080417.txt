ID Card Issue and Replacement Fees 
 

Summary/Purpose:   Defines fee schedule for University of Mississippi identification card 
   issue and replacement 
 
 
The first ID card is issued at no charge.  Replacement ID requests may be made in writing to the 
ID Center, and require a $30 fee which may be paid using Ole Miss Express, cash, check, Visa, 
or MasterCard.
