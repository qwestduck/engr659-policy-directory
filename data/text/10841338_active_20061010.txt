The University of Mississippi 

USE OF UNIVERSITY AIRCRAFT 

 

Summary / Purpose: To establish guidelines for the operation of university aircraft. 

 

University aircraft is available for official University or State of Mississippi business..  
Arrangements for the use of this aircraft can be made by contacting the university pilot or 
Procurement Services.  Use of this aircraft is on a first come, first serve basis.
