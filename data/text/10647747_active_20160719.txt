Disposal of Unknown Chemicals 
 
Summary/Purpose:  The Policy on the Disposal of Unknown Chemicals details the procedures, 
responsibilities and requirements to have Laboratory Services properly dispose of Unknown 
Chemicals. 

Unknown Chemical Disposal  

Disposal of unknown chemicals will be handled by the Laboratory Services, on a case by case 
basis, provided the department submitting the chemicals for disposal are willing to assume 
financial responsibility for the costs of the analyses required to determine the identity or 
composition of the material.
