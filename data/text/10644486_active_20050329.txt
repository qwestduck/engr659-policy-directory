Contractual Services 

 
Summary/Purpose:  Defines the roles of contracted vendors with the university  
 
 
Contractual Services Management, on behalf of the University of Mississippi, has entered into 
contracts with vendors chosen through the bid process giving them exclusive vending rights on 
campus. 
 
Other vendors are not allowed to establish services or sales locations in direct competition with 
contracted vendors.
