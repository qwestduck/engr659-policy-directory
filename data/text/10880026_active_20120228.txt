Use of Expired Medical Materials or Devices 
 
Summary/Purpose: Use of expired medical materials or devices in regard to animals in IACUC 
protocols is not permitted. 
 
Use of expired medical materials or devices in regard to animals in IACUC protocols is not 
permitted. 
 
If the PI possesses expired medical materials and/or devices, the PI must identify a segregated 
space where they will be kept (e.g., a closet, a cabinet, a shelf).  The segregated area must be 
clearly labeled as “Expired Medical Materials.”
