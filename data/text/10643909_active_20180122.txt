
The University of Mississippi 

 

Meal Plan Start Date 

Summary/Purpose: Defines meal plan start dates 

 

 

Semester meal plans may not be accessed through the participant’s ID card until 

one business day before the first day of class, provided that registration has been 

completed and processed. Summer or Intersession Term meal plans may not be 

accessed through the participant’s ID card until the first day of class, provided that 

registration has been completed and processed.
